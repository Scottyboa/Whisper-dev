Guide & Instructions

Welcome to the Whisper Transcription tool. This application allows medical professionals, therapists, and other practitioners to record and transcribe consultations, as well as generate professional notes using an AI-powered note generator.

How to Use the Functions:

  • Recording: Click "Start Recording" to begin capturing audio. Use "Stop Recording" to end and upload your recording.
  • Transcription: Once your audio is uploaded, click "Transcribe" to convert speech to text. The transcription will appear in the top area.
  • Note Generation: After transcription, use the "Generate Note" button to produce a note based on your transcription and your custom prompt. A timer shows the duration of note generation, and the generated note appears below.
  • Custom Prompt: On the right, select a prompt slot (1â€“10) and enter your custom prompt. Your prompt is saved automatically and is associated with your API key.
  • Guide Toggle: Use the sidebar buttons ("Functions" and "Guide") to switch between the tool functions and this guide. Your entered data is preserved when toggling views.

Please ensure that you have entered your API key on the index page before using the tool.
