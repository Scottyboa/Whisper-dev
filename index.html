How to Use Whisper Transcription

1. Obtain an OpenAI API Key: Sign up at OpenAI Platform to get your API key. You can add billing details and purchase credits (tokens) as needed.

2. Cost & Tokens:

  • Speech-to-text (Whisper): 0.006 USD per minute (~0.062 NOK/min).
  • Note generation is very affordable—only fractions of a cent extra per note.
  • You pay only for what you use; if you go on vacation, you pay nothing during that period.

For example, if you have 20 consultations per day, each 15 minutes long, for 4 days a week over 4 weeks, that's a total of 320 consultations (15 x 320 = 4,800 minutes per month).

Total cost for speech-to-text only: 4,800 min x 0.006 USD = 28.80 USD (~308 NOK). Adding note generation increases the cost only slightly.

Security & Confidentiality: All recordings, transcriptions, and generated notes are automatically deleted after 15 minutes. Nothing is stored permanently—your data remains confidential and secure.
